About Me

Netomi

July 2017 - Oct 2019

Netomi provides an AI-based customer support platform. My work focused on building the NLP pipeline, primarily the intent classification model and other text processing components.

Over more than two years, I iterated on various text classifiers . I eventually used a fine-tunable, Transformer-based LLM to build independent conversation pipelines for clients with very low false positives.

At that point, our approach to intent classification seemed to have "solved" the problem as we defined it.

Key Accomplishments -
  • Achieved 95%+ accuracy in intent classification with minimal domain-specific training data using Transformer-based models
  • Build an automated multi-tenant fine-tuning pipeline for intent classification models for 10+ enterprise clients (T-Mobile, Singtel, Target, Westjet and many more)
  • Reduced false positive rate to less than 2% through iterative model improvements (RNN → Graph-based → Transformer)
  • Scaled conversation pipelines to handle millions of customer interactions monthly
